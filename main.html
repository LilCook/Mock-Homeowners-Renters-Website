Brady's Insurance Guide Webpage



As the data from our survey made clear, some people did not know that renters insurance covered certain things such as loss of use, while some thought other types of natural disasters such as floods would be covered. On this webpage, you can find out what is and isn't covered by homeowners/renters insurance.

Homeowners Insurance

    What is included in Homeowners insurance:
  • Most damage claims
  • Loss of use costs (if you are unable to use the house while it is being repared)
  • Personal Liability/Theft costs
    What is not included in Homeowners insurance:
  • Any damages from floods/earthquakes
  • Any claims due to poor maintenance
  • Theft of expensive valuables (if someone steals an expensive item, it may only be partially reimbursed.)
    Who needs Homeowners insurance:

    It is recommended that anyone who owns a home should have homeowners insurance.

Renters Insurance

    What is included in Renters insurance:
  • Some natural disasters
  • Loss of use costs (if you are unable to use the house while it is being repared)
  • Personal Liability/Theft costs
    What is not included in Renters insurance:
  • Any damages from floods/earthquakes
  • If you have a roomate that is unnamed on the policy, they will not be covered.
  • Infestations of any bug/animal such as termites.
    Who needs Renters insurance:

    It is recommended that if your condominum policy does not cover many items, or if there are specific items that you want covered, then you should get renters insurance.
